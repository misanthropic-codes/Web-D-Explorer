Elon Musk

Description

Elon Reeve Musk FRS is a business magnate and investor. He is the founder, CEO, and chief engineer of SpaceX; angel investor, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; and many other ventures. Musk was born in Pretoria, South Africa, and is known for his groundbreaking innovations.

Achievements

  • 2006: Member of the United States National Academy of Sciences Aeronautics and Space Engineering Board.
  • 2007: Inducted into the design hall of fame for Tesla Roadster.
  • 2008: National Conservation Achievement Award for Tesla and SolarCity.
  • 2010: Named in Time's "100 People Who Most Affected the World".
  • 2021: Forbes ranked Musk as one of "America's 20 Most Powerful CEOs".
